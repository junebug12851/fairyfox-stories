A fable of the Salt Road. Zari, fourteen and the finest wind-seller at the Ninth Waystation, will
sell any wind to anyone with coin — which is precisely how she comes to sell the homesick wind to a
veiled stranger, and set the whole tale blowing across the Bright Waste.

_First person, past tense; cadenced and told-aloud. Five chapters. The full tale, moral and all, is
in the [blueprint](blueprint/)._
