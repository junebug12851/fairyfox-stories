A novelette of the Long Quiet. Sela Renn drafts corridors for the Cartographers' Office of the
generation ship *Ark Meridian*. Corridors don't lie. So when her measurements insist there's a
deck the master charts have never shown, she has a choice: trust the record, or trust her own two
feet.

_First person, cool and exact. Six chapters. The full plan, including where the ship is really
going, is in the [blueprint](blueprint/)._
