A caper of the Gaslight Reach. Juno Vale can find anything and lift most of it, which is exactly the
sort of reputation that gets a person into trouble. When a client offers her a fortune to steal a
curio out of the Ordinal's own auction house — and proves they know the secret that could hang her
— saying no stops being an option.

_First person, past tense, and quick on its feet. Planned at seven chapters; grown a chapter at a
time. The whole plan, twists and ending included, is in the [blueprint](blueprint/) — read it if
you like knowing the shape of a trap before you step in it._
