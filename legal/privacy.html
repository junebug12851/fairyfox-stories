Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

The short version. This policy is for the Fairy Fox Stories collection (not the wider Fairy Fox site). It has no accounts and collects no personal information about you. Your reading progress and reading/display preferences are saved only in your own browser. We use no cookies, no analytics, and no tracking, and we serve our own fonts so nothing is loaded from a third-party font service.

This Privacy Policy explains how Fairy Fox Stories (“the Service”, “we”, “us”) handles information. Fairy Fox Stories is a free, open-source collection of short interactive stories created by 1fairyfox — one project within the wider Fairy Fox site. It is played on the web at fairyfox.io/fairyfox-stories/. The stories run entirely in your browser. Other Fairy Fox projects have their own policies; this one covers only the games collection.

What we do not collect

We built the Service so that we collect as little as possible — in practice, nothing about you on our side. Specifically:

  • There are no user accounts and no sign-up. We never ask for your name, email, phone number, or address.
  • We run no analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or behavioral profiling, and we do not build any user database.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or location.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with anyone, because we do not hold any.

Information stored on your own device

To make the games nicer to play, the Service saves a little information in your browser — not on our servers. This is your reading progress for each game and your reading and display preferences (the “Aa” reading menu’s theme, accent colour, text size, line spacing, and width). It is stored using your browser’s local storage and stays on your device; it is never transmitted to us. The reading preferences are saved under a shared key so a single choice carries across the wider fairyfox.io site in the same browser — this is still local to your device only. You can delete any of it at any time by clearing your browser’s site data for the Service, which simply resets your saved reading progress and preferences.

Hosting and server logs

The site is served as static files by GitHub Pages. Like virtually all web hosting, the provider automatically processes basic technical information required to deliver the site and keep it secure — most notably your IP address and standard request metadata — in its server logs. This is handled by GitHub as our hosting provider under its own privacy policy. We do not add our own analytics, profiles, or user database on top of it.

Fonts and third-party requests

The Service’s fonts are served by the Service itself, from its own address. They are not loaded from Google Fonts or any other third-party font service, so no information about you (such as your IP address) is sent to a font provider when a page loads. The games embed no third-party scripts, ad networks, or social widgets.

Cookies

The Service does not use cookies. It uses your browser’s local storage for your reading progress and preferences as described above, which is not a tracking cookie and is not transmitted to us. See our Cookies Policy for details.

Children’s privacy

Fairy Fox Stories is a general-audience collection of simple, non-violent games suitable for all ages. Because we do not collect personal information from anyone, we do not knowingly collect it from children. If you believe a child has sent us personal information — for example, by emailing us — please contact us and we will delete it.

Your privacy rights (GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and others)

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information, to object to or restrict its processing, and to not be discriminated against for exercising those rights. Because we hold essentially no personal information about you, there is usually nothing on our side to access or delete — the data that exists (your reading progress and preferences) is on your own device and is fully under your control (clear it at any time as described above).

For California residents (CCPA/CPRA): we do not collect, sell, or “share” (for cross-context behavioral advertising) your personal information, and we do not use sensitive personal information.

For users in the EEA/UK (GDPR): where any limited processing occurs (such as the hosting logs described above), it is based on our legitimate interest in operating and securing the Service. You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the Service secure, but no method of storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure. The stories run client-side and we operate no server that holds your data.

International users

The Service can be used worldwide. When you load it, your request and the limited technical data described above may be processed in the United States or other countries where our hosting operates.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the Service changes. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Significant changes will be reflected here on this page.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at fairy@fairyfox.io.
