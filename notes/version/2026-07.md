  draw for you."* Sela decides nothing the day she learns its size ("that is not a decision, it is a
  flinch") — but sees what her trade always was: someone draws Deck Nought as solid hull, by hand, every
  generation, knowing. And it's about to be her. Sets up the finale.

## (maintenance) — `branch-sync` no longer fails on every release

The back-merge invariant guard triggers on push to `main` and checked `dev` instantly — but the
back-merge lands a few seconds *after* the merge, by design, so the job always ran while `dev` was
legitimately one commit behind and failed. (It failed on the v0.3.4 release and passed on re-run —
a race, not drift.) It now retries six times at 30s intervals before failing: a genuinely skipped
back-merge is still caught, just ~2.5 minutes later. CI only; no change to the site.
