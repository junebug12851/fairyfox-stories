## Post-release verification — the coins store key (`:a` vs `:b`)

Owner flagged a concern that "the store uses `:b` not `:a`." Ran it down against the master rather than
find-replacing: refreshed the reference mirror (hub `0.20.2 → 0.20.4`) and checked every ref.

- **The coins store key is `fairyfox:coins:a`** on the latest master (hub 0.20.4) — coins.js is unchanged
  since 0.19.1, well before our adoption, so our vendored copy is current. `fairyfox:coins:b` **never
  appears anywhere in the hub's history**.
- The **`:b`** store is the **reader** menu (`fairyfox:reader:b`) — a different, already-shipped key. The
  two version letters are independent (reader `:b`, coins `:a`); the concern was the reader key.
- **Not changed, deliberately.** The coins key is a shared, vendored-verbatim value — the wallet is shared
  across every same-origin fairyfox.io site by that one key. Forking Stories to `:b` alone would desync the
  shared wallet and violate the do-not-alter rule. A real key bump is a **hub** change (bump the master
  coins.js with an `:a → :b` migration), which then flows to every project — not a Stories-only edit.
- Also confirmed the 0.20.2→0.20.4 hub delta carries **nothing Stories adopts** (no standards changed;
  coins.js / main.css unchanged; the new commits are hub-catalog onboarding — DespawnedItems, PaperMC — plus
  a maintenance round-up). Reference mirror left fast-forwarded to `0.20.4`. Also repointed the `origin`
  remote to the canonical `1fairyfox/fairyfox-stories` URL (was resolving via the rename redirect).
